# Intl Overview

- `Intl.NumberFormat` for currency, percentages, compact notation.
- `Intl.DateTimeFormat` for localized dates and times.
- `Intl.RelativeTimeFormat` to say "3 days ago" or "in 2 hours".
- `Intl.Collator` for locale-aware sorting of strings.
- `Intl.ListFormat` and `Intl.PluralRules` for user-friendly messages.
